Privacy Policy for Ask ANANTA

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Ask ANANTA ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Ask ANANTA mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Chat Messages: We store your chat conversations with our AI assistant locally on your device to provide contextual responses and maintain chat history
  • Chat Names: We store custom chat names you create for organizing your conversations locally on your device
  • Kundali Information: If you choose to use the Kundali feature, birth details (date, time, place, gender) are stored locally on your device
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our app, including features used and session duration

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, and device identifiers
  • Advertising ID: Google Advertising ID (AAID) is collected by Google AdMob for advertising purposes
  • Usage Analytics: App performance data, crash reports, and feature usage statistics collected by AdMob
  • Session Data: Chat session timestamps and conversation flow

Data Sent to Third-Party Services

  • AI Processing: Your questions are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate AI responses. This data is processed by Google's servers.
  • Ad Serving: Advertising data (including Advertising ID) is shared with Google AdMob for ad delivery and measurement.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Core App Functionality

  • Chat Management: To maintain your chat history locally and provide contextual responses
  • AI Responses: To send your questions to Google Gemini API and receive spiritual guidance responses
  • Session Management: To create new chat sessions and manage conversation flow
  • Memory Management: To prevent data overflow and optimize app performance
  • Kundali Feature: To generate personalized spiritual readings based on birth details you provide

Advertising and Marketing

  • Ad Display: To show relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Ad Performance: To measure ad effectiveness and optimize ad delivery
  • Ad Personalization: To show personalized ads based on your interests (using Advertising ID)

App Improvement

  • Feature Development: To understand user needs and improve app functionality
  • Performance Optimization: To enhance app speed and reliability
  • Bug Fixes: To identify and resolve technical issues through crash reports

User Experience

  • Personalization: To provide relevant spiritual guidance based on your questions
  • Chat Organization: To help you manage and access your conversation history
  • Quick Questions: To provide helpful suggestions for new users

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • SQLite Database: Your chat data, chat names, and Kundali information are stored locally on your device
  • SharedPreferences: App settings and user preferences are stored locally
  • Local-Only Data: Chat history, Kundali details, and app settings remain on your device

Third-Party Data Processing

  • Google Gemini API: Your questions are transmitted to Google's servers for AI processing. Google processes this data according to their privacy policy.
  • Google AdMob: Advertising data (including Advertising ID) is processed by Google AdMob according to Google's advertising policies.

Security Measures

  • Local Encryption: Chat data stored locally is encrypted using Android's built-in security features
  • HTTPS Transmission: All data transmitted to third-party services uses encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Device-Only Access: Only you can access your locally stored chat data on your device

The Android Advertising ID (AAID) is a unique, user-resettable identifier for advertising, provided by Google Play services.
